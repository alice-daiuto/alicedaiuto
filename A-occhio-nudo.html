E. GALLIANI, P. PASSCUZZI G. POLIMENO
/ 2022
/ EXHIBITED AT TOURIN GRAPHIC DAYS 2023

Transmedia translation of Blindness, a novel by Jos√© Saramago.
The project was created with the intention of rewriting the story by involving, through the use of various graphic devices, the only sense missing from the narrative: sight. the reader, not only follows on several levels what blindness narrates, but is also guided by it through pages that simulate the asphyxiation and suffocating sensations of the events that punctuate the story. To do this, we experimented with graphics and typography, often manipulating them to create the desired visual effects.
