SACRI MONTI

/ BRANDING
/ UNIVERSITY PROJECT
/ 2022
/ TEAM: A. D'AIUTO, E. GALLIANI, C. SERENI,
A.F. VITALI, L.M. ZIGLIOLI

Rebranding of the Unesco site ‘Sacri Monti of Piedmont and Lombardy’.
Sacri Monti are nine groups of devotional chapels and other architectural elements erected between the late 15th and early 18th century, dedicated to various aspects of the Catholic faith.
