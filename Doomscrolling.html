DOOMSCROLLING

/ ANIMATED POSTERS
/ UNIVERSITY PROJECT
/ 2024
/ TEAM: A. D'AIUTO, T. MAUKO
/ DISPLAYED AT "OH, SHIFT!" EXHIBITION IN GRAZ

This project aims to explore the phenomenon of doomscrolling related to climate-anxiety, through a series of three posters, each dedicated to a specific stage of this behavior: The Hook, The Line, and The Sinker.
The choice of a dark and messy visual language aims to convey the intensity and emotional complexity of this phenomenon. The Hook aims to capture the viewer's attention, mirroring the effect of captivating headlines. The Line gives the illusion of endless scrolling of catastrophical news we read everyday. The Sinker depicts emotional investment and the difficulty of detaching through visuals that evoke a sense of entrapment and confusion.
