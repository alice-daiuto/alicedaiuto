POST MEMORIE

/ EDITORIAL DESIGN & MIXED MEDIA VIDEO
/ UNIVERSITY WORKSHOP
/ 2024
/ TEAM: A. CASACA, A. CUPISTI, A. D'AIUTO,
E. GALLIANI G. POLIMENO

“What would you like to be forgotten about you?” Post Memories is a collection of answers to this seemingly simple question that addresses the complex issue of fear of oblivion. The narrative is interwoven with visual experimentation and interpretive work, investigating how these memories can be erased. People often end up identifying with those memories, overemphasizing their importance.
The project also includes a video trailer made with the mixed media technique, offering a unique visual representation of the stories collected.
