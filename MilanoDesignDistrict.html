MILANO DESIGN DISTRICT

/ BRANDING
/ CONTEST BY AIAP
/ TEAM: A. D'AIUTO, C. SERENI
/ 2023

Brand identity proposal for Milano Design District, an association of the main design flagship stores in Milan.
The dynamism of the logo is intended to represent the evolution of the district, but also of design and of the city of Milan. The choice of colour palette and the system of illustrations of iconic objects evoke the history of Milanese design.
