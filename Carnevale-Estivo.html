CARNEVALE ESTIVO

/ BRANDING & SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGEMENT
/ FREELENCE WORK
/ 2023, 2024

↘ Link to IG profile

Brand design and communication for the 11th and 12th editions of the event "Carnevale Estivo di Laurenzana". The visual identity reflects the innovative and audacious spirit of the event, through the use of bright colours, bold typography and moving geometric shapes. The goal is to convey the essence, energy, and fun of the event while ensuring visual consistency across all materials, including the official mascots.
I was also in charge of the social media management, developing a strategic editorial plan and creating multimedia content (posts, stories, reels) to consolidate the brand in people's mind, to engage the audience and to promote the event.
